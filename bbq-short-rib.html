BBQ Short Ribs Recipes

Description:

BBQ short ribs are a beloved dish known for their rich, meaty flavor and tender texture, achieved through slow cooking or grilling.

Here’s a detailed description of a classic BBQ short ribs recipe that balances sweetness and tanginess, resulting in a mouth-watering meal that's perfect for gatherings or a family dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 lbs beef short ribs: Choose ribs with a good amount of marbling for the best flavor and tenderness.
  • BBQ Sauce: You can use a store-bought variety or make your own. A basic homemade sauce might include ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and a dash of hot sauce.
  • Salt and pepper: For basic seasoning of the ribs before cooking.
  • Optional marinade ingredients: Soy sauce, honey, and a touch of sesame oil for an Asian-inspired flavor.
  • Additional spices: Such as cumin, chili powder, or a BBQ spice rub to enhance the meat’s flavor.

Cooking Steps:

  1. Preparation:

    • Season the Ribs: Pat the ribs dry with paper towels and season generously with salt, pepper, and any additional spices or a BBQ rub. For deeper flavor, you can marinate the ribs for several hours or overnight in the refrigerator with a mixture of BBQ sauce and optional marinade ingredients.

  2. Pre-Cooking:

    • Oven Method: Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).

      Place the ribs in a baking dish, cover tightly with foil, and bake for about 2-3 hours. This slow cooking method helps tenderize the meat.

    • Instant Pot Method: For a quicker method, you can cook the short ribs in an Instant Pot on high pressure for about 45 minutes with a cup of beef broth. Release the pressure naturally.

  3. Grilling:

    • Preheat the Grill:
    • After pre-cooking, preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • Grill the Ribs: Brush the ribs with BBQ sauce and grill them for 3-5 minutes on each side until they are nicely charred and glazed.

      Continuously apply BBQ sauce while turning the ribs to build up a rich layer of flavor.

  4. Serving:

    • Rest the Ribs: Let the ribs rest for a few minutes after grilling to allow the juices to redistribute
    • Serve: Serve the BBQ short ribs with extra BBQ sauce on the side and typical accompaniments such as coleslaw, cornbread, and grilled vegetables.
